## Checklist Format

Use this checklist to make your decision:

### Step 1: Analyze Your Use Case
- [ ] Defined the problem clearly
- [ ] Identified success metrics
- [ ] Mapped stakeholders

### Step 2: Evaluate Against Criteria
- [ ] Criterion 1: [Option A / Option B]
- [ ] Criterion 2: [Option A / Option B]
- [ ] Criterion 3: [Option A / Option B]

### Step 3: Make Decision
- [ ] Majority of criteria favor: [Option chosen]
- [ ] Decision documented with reasoning
- [ ] Stakeholders aligned on choice

### Step 4: Validate
- [ ] Pilot with chosen option
- [ ] Results confirm decision
- [ ] Adjust if needed
